The WinWinX Payment Protection Plan
Sell with absolute peace of mind, knowing that WinWinX has got your back every step of the way! Our Payment Protection Plans are tailored specifically for Sellers like you, ensuring smooth transactions and providing a safety net for your valuable products and services. It’s a genuine Win-Win for everyone involved!
To benefit from this great protection, simply follow these easy steps while creating your Product Listing:
  • Navigate to the second page of “Sell Now” and scroll down.
  • At the bottom, you’ll find the option to “Add a Payment Protection Plan.“
  • Select “Yes” and let the magic begin!
Now, here’s an important detail to consider: As the Seller, you’ll pay a flat fee of 10% of the total product price at the beginning of the transaction. The 10% fee is deducted from the Buyer’s Down Payment and NOT a separate payment you have to make. This fee serves as a guarantee for the remaining installments, should the Buyer fail to make them. Once the transaction commences, you’ll receive the Buyer’s Down Payment minus the cost of the Payment Protection Plan.
Once your Product/Service is sold, and the contract takes effect, the Payment Protection Plan automatically kicks into action, safeguarding your earnings! In the unlikely event that you encounter any difficulties in receiving funds from the Buyer during the transaction, WinWinX will step in and reimburse you the installment(s) amount under the following circumstances:

The Fine Print
Introduction:
By purchasing a Payment Protection Plan, hereinafter referred to as “PPP the Seller agrees to the terms and conditions set forth by WinWinX, Inc., hereinafter referred to as “The Company.“ The Policy aims to provide reimbursement to the Seller for payments not received from the Buyer, as outlined in the following sections.
Eligibility:
  • 2.1 The Seller must be a registered participant on the WinWinX platform and have a valid and active account.
  • 2.2 The Seller must adhere to all terms and conditions specified in the WinWinX user agreement.
Coverage:
  • 3.1 The Company will reimburse the Seller for payments outstanding from the Buyer as specified in the transaction details on the WinWinX platform.
  • 3.2 Reimbursement will be made under the condition that the Seller has fulfilled their obligations in accordance with the terms and conditions of the WinWinX platform.
  • 3.3 The Company reserves the right to assess the validity of the claim and may request supporting documentation from the Seller to verify the outstanding payments.
  • 3.4 The maximum reimbursement amount per transaction shall not exceed the amount specified in the transaction details on the WinWinX platform.
Reimbursement Process:
  • 4.1 In the event of outstanding payments, the Seller must notify the Company within 30 days from the due date specified in the transaction details.
  • 4.2 The Seller shall provide all relevant information and supporting documentation requested by the Company to assess the claim.
  • 4.3 The Company will review the claim and, if approved, reimburse the Seller within 60 days from the date of claim submission.
  • 4.4 The reimbursement shall be made via electronic transfer or any other method deemed appropriate by the Company.
Transfer of Rights and Recovery:
  • 5.1 Upon reimbursement, the Seller assigns to the Company all rights, title, and interest in the outstanding payments from the Buyer.
  • 5.2 The Company assumes full responsibility for recovering the outstanding payments from the Buyer.
  • 5.3 The Company may pursue legal action or employ debt collection agencies to recover the outstanding payments, and/or repossession of product or service value sold by Seller as it deems necessary.
Exclusions:
  • 6.1 PPP does not cover any payments disputed or deemed fraudulent by the Buyer.
  • 6.2 PPP does not cover any losses resulting from the Seller’s failure to comply with the terms and conditions of the WinWinX platform.
  • 6.3 PPP does not cover any losses resulting from intentional or willful misconduct by the Seller.
  • 6.4 PPP does not cover consumable products or services.
Termination:
  • 7.1 The Seller may terminate this Policy by providing written notice to the Company, subject to any ongoing claims under review.
  • 7.2 The Company may terminate this Policy at its sole discretion by providing written notice to the Seller.
  • 7.3 In the event of termination, any pending claims shall be resolved based on the terms and conditions applicable at the time of the claim.
Modifications:
The Company reserves the right to modify the terms and conditions of this Policy at any time, with prior notice to the Seller. The modified terms and conditions shall be applicable to all new claims submitted after the effective date of the modification.
